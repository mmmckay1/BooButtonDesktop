# Privacy Policy for Boo Button

**Effective date:** July 18, 2026

Boo Button ("the app") is a simple app with one button. When you press it, it plays a sound. That's the entire app.

## Data Collection

Boo Button does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or device information. The app:

- Does not require an account or login
- Does not connect to the internet or any server
- Does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind
- Does not access your microphone, camera, contacts, location, or files
- Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

## Permissions

The app plays a bundled sound file when you press the button. On Android, this requires only the standard audio-playback system permission; no microphone or recording permission is requested.

## Children's Privacy

Since the app collects no data from anyone, it does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the relevant age in your jurisdiction).

## Changes to This Policy

If this app is ever updated in a way that changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated accordingly and the effective date above will change.
